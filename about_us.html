About Our Collage

The Capital Highway Training College (CHTC) was established in the year 2022 with the aim of providing quality education and training to students. It is located in Rumuruti town, in Laikipia County. Since its inception, CHTC has been dedicated to offering a wide range of diploma, certificate, and artisan courses to cater to the educational needs of aspiring students.

CHTC takes pride in its team of professionals who come from diverse disciplines, bringing a wealth of knowledge and expertise to the institution. The college believes in the power of a multidisciplinary approach to education, which enables students to gain insights from various fields and develop a well-rounded skill set.

With a commitment to creating a conducive learning environment, CHTC has invested in quality facilities and equipment. These resources are designed to support students' learning journeys and provide them with hands-on experiences that align with industry standards.

Goals

1. Enhance Academic Excellence: CHTC aims to continually improve the quality of education by promoting academic excellence in all its programs. This includes updating curriculum, employing experienced faculty, and implementing effective teaching methods.

2. Foster Skill Development: CHTC is committed to providing students with practical skills and knowledge that are relevant to the business world. The college will focus on incorporating industry-specific training, internships, and practical experiences to enhance students' employability.


3. Promote Innovation and Research: CHTC aims to foster a culture of innovation and research among students and faculty. This includes encouraging research projects, organizing seminars and conferences, and promoting entrepreneurial initiatives within the college.

Our Mission

The Capital Highway Training College aims at providing extensive knowledge and producing competent students. The college is dedicated to nurturing individuals who are well-prepared to excel in their chosen fields and contribute effectively to society. By offering comprehensive programs, CHTC strives to equip students with the necessary skills and practical experience to succeed in the professional world.

Our Vision

The Capital Highway Training College aspires to provide a wide range of frameworks and purpose in the business world. It envisions becoming a leading institution that not only imparts knowledge but also instills a sense of purpose and entrepreneurship in its students. By fostering innovation, research, and ethical practices, CHTC aims to shape future leaders who can make a positive impact in the business landscape.

Admissions and Registrations
